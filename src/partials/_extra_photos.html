Underwater video taken at Van Damme State Park (Mendocino County) in August 2016 shows bare rock reef where there should be lots of bull kelp and other seaweed growing, which normally feed abalone and red sea urchin. Warm ocean temperatures and an invasion of purple sea urchin have decimated the kelp forest, leaving the abalone and red urchin starving.
Video: Athena Maguire, California Department of Fish and Wildlife.
